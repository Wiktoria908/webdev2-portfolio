Messenger

Messenger is a messaging app platform developed by Facebook, Messenger is easy to use, you can connect with Facebook friends and contacts on your phone. Messenger can be downloaded from the Appstore and you set up your account with your phone number or Facebook. Some of the more common risks include cyberbullying, experiencing or sharing inappropriate content and chatting with strangers.
