About us

As part of the Web Development project we were asked to choose at least five different social media tools and then create a page about them. Firstly we were put into groups where each group had to research a different social media tool of their choice. Then we had to go off on our own and create a website using our knowledge of web development that we have learned.

I chose to do my website on Messenger, Whatsapp, Instagram, Tik Tok and Facebook since I use them all frequently and find them to be the most popular amongst people. They are very handy to use and have many different features. I created this website to teach people about these social media tools so anyone could use them and to make it easier for them to understand their purpose. So go and enjoy!
